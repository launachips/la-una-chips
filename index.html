About Us

—

La Una Natural Products is a Sukabumi-based food manufacturer specializing in natural, crispy nankhwa made from premium local ingredients. Our core principles: Fresh made to Order, purely Natural, Superior Texture, and Quality Ingredients.

We are committed to providing premium Indonesian snacks to Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

Fresh Made to Order

Every product is made fresh upon order to ensure maximum freshness and quality.

Purely Natural

No artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. Just pure, natural ingredients.

Superior Texture

Perfectly crispy texture that stays crunchy longer with our special recipe.

Quality Ingredients

Sourced directly from local farmers and suppliers who share our values.
